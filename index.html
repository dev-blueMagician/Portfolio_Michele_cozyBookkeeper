Hello There!
I am passionate about helping small businesses organize and streamline their books so they can focus on doing what they do best: Running their business. I've been doing this for over 30 years and I value each new client as a challenge, with unique needs and goals. I've had extensive experience working for various CPA firms in Detroit and Los Angeles as well as Business Management Firms in Los Angeles. I help small to medium size businesses clean up the mess, get caught up, and stay current. Imagine how that would feel??

As a Certified Life Coach, I am gifted with a knack for helping clients reframe their minds for personal and professional growth. These two skillsets (bookkeeping and coaching) complement each other and provide added benefits to you for my services.

In my spare time, I strive for work-life-balance by enjoying and playing music, watching and reading sci-fi, pampering my animals, animal rescue, chasing Auroras, and pursuing arts and crafts.
